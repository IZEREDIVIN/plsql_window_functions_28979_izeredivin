## Business Problem
An online retail company needs to analyze customer purchases, product performance, and sales trends in order to make better marketing and sales decisions.

---

## Success Criteria
1. Identify top products per region using ranking window functions
2. Compute running totals of monthly sales
3. Analyze month-to-month sales growth
4. Segment customers into performance groups
5. Calculate moving averages for sales trends
